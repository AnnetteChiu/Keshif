Keshif * Keşif (keshif) means discovery and exploration in Turkish.
Data Made Explorable
Explore and understand your data - interactively, visually, rapidly, and easily .
Explore sample datasets below using Keshif | Learn with 5-minute tutorial | Explore your data | Get the code
  • Create summaries of data properties. Keshif will present your data in its effective visual design.
  • Discover relations in your data rapidly. Highlight relations by mouse over. Compare records by locking your selection. Keshif synchronizes all selections.
  • Enjoy scalability by design. Keshif avoids visualization techniques that cannot scale. Explore data as long as it can be fully loaded to your web browser.
  • Enjoy consistency. Keshif solves your exploration needs for a wide range of datasets in a single unified visual and interactive design.
  • Focus on your data and your questions. Don't worry about picking the right visualization and doing the math. Keshif does it for you.
  • See the overview and the most relevant first. After each step, the most relevant results and data properties are shown.
  • Load your structured data in a flexible pipeline, built for the web. Keshif can automatically import data from Google Sheets and CSV/TEXT files. You can write custom data importers in JavaScript to load JSON, XML, HTML, or even BibTeX. Check the demo listing to find demos using various data resource types.
  • Define your browser using a simple configuration API, including the data source, the properties to summaries, and how to display records individually.
